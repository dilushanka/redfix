Signs You Need a New Display

Not sure if it’s time to replace your phone screen? Look out for these warning signs:

1. Cracks or Shattered Glass

Even small cracks can worsen and cause touch issues over time.

2. Flickering or Lines

If your display shows lines, flickers, or unusual colors, it may be failing.

3. Unresponsive Touch

When parts of your screen stop responding, it’s usually a sign of damage.

4. Black Spots or Dead Pixels

Dark patches or missing pixels reduce usability and indicate screen damage.

5. Screen Separation

If the display starts lifting from the frame, it’s time for a replacement.
