5 Tips to Extend Your Phone Battery Life

Battery draining too fast? Here are 5 proven tips from RedFix to keep your phone lasting longer:

1. Reduce Screen Brightness

High brightness is one of the biggest battery drainers. Use auto-brightness or reduce it manually.

2. Turn Off Background Apps

Apps running in the background consume power. Close unused apps regularly.

3. Disable Location & Bluetooth

Only turn on GPS, Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi when needed. These features constantly drain energy.

4. Enable Battery Saver Mode

Modern smartphones have built-in battery saver modes that reduce background usage and notifications.

5. Avoid Overcharging

Charging overnight or past 100% can reduce battery health over time. Unplug once fully charged.
