What To Do If Your Phone Gets Wet

Water damage is one of the most common phone issues. Here’s what you should do immediately:

1. Power Off Immediately

Don’t try to check if it still works. Turn it off to prevent short circuits.

2. Remove Accessories

Take out SIM cards, memory cards, and cases to allow air circulation.

3. Dry with a Soft Cloth

Wipe away visible water, but do not use heat sources like a hair dryer.

4. Do Not Charge

Charging a wet phone can cause permanent damage. Wait until it’s fully dry.
